WELCOME!


Hi there! Do you like little critters or cute animals? Well, you've come to the right place! In this website, you'll be able to take care of your very own digital pet! Feed, play, and take care of your pet and make sure it doesn't run away!

HOW TO PLAY

To play this game, you're gonna have to follow some rules and understand the mechanics!

  1. Make sure to keep track of your critter's food, hygine, rest, and play levels. If they go bellow 0, your pet will run away to find a better owner.

  2. Make your critter's food in the kitchen (later to be added) and make sure the recipies will be followed or your critter might end up losing food points.

  3. When you want to play with your critter, you should only use things classified as "toys" or your critter will lose play points.

  4. Make sure to give your critter a bath, brush its teeth (if it has teeth), and make it looks and feels cute! Your pet might get stinky or feel sad if they don't look their best.

  5. Your critter will lose rest points for every activity you do with it. Go to the bedroom (to be added) to make sure it gets a good nights sleep!
